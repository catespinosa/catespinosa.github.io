Rueda de casino

Play tracks

Pairs of dancers form a circle, with dance moves called out by one person, a caller. “Calls” in a Rueda include turns patterns, footwork sequences, and various games.

Son cubano

Play tracks

Son is danced on the two count (contratiempo). It combines the structure and feel of the Spanish canción with Afro-Cuban traits and percussion.


Rumba

Play tracks

Vocal improvisation, elaborate dancing and polyrhythmic drumming are the key components of all rumba styles (yambú, guaguancó and columbia). Cajones (wooden boxes) were used as drums until the early 20th century, when they were replaced by tumbadoras (conga drums).
